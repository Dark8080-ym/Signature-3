What members get

Support categories with full coverage

This layout keeps the required service information while adding real images and cleaner structure.

  1. Transportation Support

    Transportation help keeps members on schedule and removes extra friction around training days.

    • Weekday Shuttle Assistance — Monday through Friday, 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM
    • Weekend Local Transit Support — Saturday, 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM
    • Route Information Desk — Daily, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM

  2. Maintenance Services

    Routine maintenance keeps equipment, locker spaces, and common areas safe and ready for use.

    • Daily Equipment Inspection — Monday through Friday, 5:30 AM to 8:30 AM
    • Locker and Facility Repairs — Tuesday and Thursday, 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM
    • Preventive Maintenance Checks — Saturday, 7:00 AM to 11:00 AM

  3. Cleaning & Sanitation

    Cleaning runs throughout the day to support a professional, comfortable training environment.

    • Morning Floor and Equipment Cleaning — Daily, 4:30 AM to 6:00 AM
    • Midday Sanitation Touch-Up — Monday through Friday, 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM
    • Locker Room Deep Clean — Daily, 8:00 PM to 10:00 PM

  4. Training & Coaching Support

    Coaching access includes consultations, small-group sessions, mobility support, and youth programs.

    • Personal Training Consultations — Monday through Saturday, 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM
    • Small Group Strength Classes — Monday, Wednesday, and Friday, 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM
    • Recovery and Mobility Guidance — Tuesday and Thursday, 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM
